# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project should be a respectful and productive place for learning,
experimentation, and technical discussion.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Be respectful when discussing code.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Explain technical disagreements clearly.
- Be patient with beginners.
- Focus criticism on the code, design, or idea, not the person.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Insults, discrimination, or hostile comments
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests,
or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you notice unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately.
